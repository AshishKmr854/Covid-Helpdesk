EMERGENCY MEDICAL SERVICES

Emergency medical service (EMS) also known as ambulance services provide urgent medical care on the scene. To respond to different types of medical emergencies various types of ambulances are designed which are provided with special medical equipment and medical professionals who can deal with medical emergencies. Meddco ambulance assistance app can help choose the best kind of ambulances from basic ambulances to air ambulance they have simple to the most advanced type of modern ambulances to select and transport patients and act as the First responder.



Basic Ambulance

These types of ambulances are the basic ambulances that we commonly see in our daily lives. Basic ambulances are handled by an emergency medical technician (EMT) and transport patients who require basic medical supervision under minor or uncritical situations such as mild fractures, and sub-acute care facilities (nursing homes). It comprises of the patient bed, pulse oximetry and oxygen delivery devices.
